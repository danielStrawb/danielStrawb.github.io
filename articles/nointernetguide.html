Ways to become independent

Google

Supporting Google - it's supporting not only domination in search engines, but also other services like eMail, video hosting. This company makes so much to make you depend on it's, and see every person use it. The best, that you can do - it's store your personal data on local storages:

  • external HDD,
  • USB flash drive
  • physical storages, like books, and notepads

YouTube

After you are copy all your data to one of this storages, now we should change our YouTube channels and playlists, and i have a few good ways:

  • Invidious
  • Piped
  • youtube-dl

I try all of this methods, and can say, that the best way, it's download videos to your HDD with youtube-dl, because it's minimal way. But, maybe not for all. I use YouTube last years rather as archive of my favorite channels and live perforamnces, musical videos, but maybe you are use it for fun, so i recommend use software like FreeTube for computer, and LibreTube for Android.

if you wanna to download YouTube channels, i use command like this:

yt-dlp --geo-bypass --yes-playlist --write-description --write-thumbnail --write-subs --sub-format srt -o "%(uploader)s/%(playlist)s/%(upload_date)s - %(title)s.%(ext)s" -f 'bestvideo+bestaudio' --prefer-ffmpeg --download-archive .archive.txt {url}

and this for playlists:

yt-dlp --geo-bypass --yes-playlist --write-description --write-thumbnail --write-subs --sub-format srt -o "%(uploader)s/%(playlist)s/%(playlist_index)s - %(title)s.%(ext)s" -f 'bestvideo+bestaudio' --prefer-ffmpeg --download-archive .archive.txt {url}

After, you have a very good strustured directory with your favorite Youtube videos!

Websites

It's great to have websites on hard drive, to acess interesting and important information without Internet-connection. Not all websites can be download with full functional, but you can use wget for this:

wget --tries 10 -N --continue --progres=bar --force-directories --no-check-certificate --recursive --convert-links --mirror --page-requisites --strict-comments --execute robots="off" {url}

Also, you can add -i ./websites.txt, to download a few websites in one command, but sometimes it can cause a little problems with link redirect.
