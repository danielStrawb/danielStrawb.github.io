I want to show you some view on guitar tabulature (or simply tabs), i think, that in future i make more articles about musical theory.

What wrong with tabs? Or this is good?

There is nothing wrong with tabs, it's a good way to make guitar easier, you don't need to learn musical notes, and note sheets to just play some songs, but many peoples thinks, that this is enough, and don't have progression in music, but they are have a good perspective.

You don't understand music, if you are play with tabs

When you are play music by tabs, you don't remember pregression of song, you just read numbers, you don't understand, how guitaris of you'r band make this riff/solo/chords. And honestly, i have notepad with tabs, to play, when i disconnected from electricity and Internet, and i cannot hear a song.
