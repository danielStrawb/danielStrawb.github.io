Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc

Is this another visual novel? Yes, but there is something unique to Danganronpa, than usual games in this genre. Comparing it to Katawa Shoujo or Doki-Doki, I'd rather compare it to Persona. To be honest. I have never completed any of these games, unlike Danganronpa.

I am really excited, how they are to combine 3D, and 2D pixel art pictures, and put them into a detective, visual novel, shooter and dating simulator.

The main character is Makoto Naegi, the Ultimate (un)Lucky Student, who is just an ordinary guy, who is invited to Hope's Peak Academy along with other Ultimate Students.

All of these characters were made to inspire you. While I play this video game, I learn some important life lessons throught dialogues, become more self-controlled, or simply find love :D

Their stories are truly inspiring.

In a few days, I will start living on my own, studying at an academy, and sharing an apartments with two friends. I understand that co-living can be a good idea, as long as everyone else has their own space and freedom.

This is an hour full of fascinating stories... This is Danganronpa!
